About Me

My work rests on three pillars: Communication, Clarity, and Problem-Solving.

For too long, I was asked to be a screwdriver when I’m really a Swiss Army knife: versatile, adaptable, and designed to connect people, technology, and ideas.

When you work with me, you don’t just get someone who can teach lipreading or BSL, guide digital skills, or fix technical issues. You get someone who sees the bigger picture - and helps turn complexity into clarity, overwhelm into calm, and problems into solutions.

My career spans IT infrastructure, accessibility consulting, construction, and education, giving me rare insight into how people and systems interact and how to make them work better together. I bring a unique blend of IT expertise, communication skills, and accessibility awareness. My experience includes digital literacy training, deaf education support, and hands-on technical problem-solving. Exceptional visual communication and pattern recognition skills enhance both my technical and people-focused work.

I don’t fit into one box - I build the bridges between them.

This isn’t just what I do. It’s who I am.
