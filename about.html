About Me

Who I Am

I’m Ryan Eaton, a Communication & Tech Support Specialist. I bring together human warmth with technical expertise, helping people feel more confident and in control in their everyday lives.

What I Do

I support people 1:1 in three main areas: communication, clarity, and problem-solving. Whether it’s lipreading, BSL, digital literacy, or navigating systems, I focus on making things accessible and stress-free.

Why It Matters

I believe in empowering communication, supporting clarity, and solving problems. My work connects worlds that don’t often meet — Deaf and hearing, human and tech, simple and complex — always with compassion and clarity.
