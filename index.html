Communication Support — Compassionate, Clear, Human

I help you build confidence in how you express yourself and connect with others. Whether you’re Deaf, neurodiverse, or just feeling lost in the noise, I offer practical lipreading tips, body language insights, and basic BSL — real communication that works for you.

Tech Support — Calm, Friendly, Hassle-Free

Technology can be overwhelming, but I’m here to help. I provide straightforward, jargon-free support to simplify your digital life — from setting up devices to troubleshooting and helping you feel in control. No sales pitches, just honest, approachable tech help.

Everyday Support — Real-World Guidance, Your Way

Sometimes you just need someone dependable and clear-headed. I offer grounded support to help you tackle day-to-day challenges, understand systems, and gain the confidence to speak up or try something new. No jargon, no pressure — just practical help that fits your life.

Digital Literacy — Online Confidence, Real Life Skills

The Internet should work for you, not against you. I offer clear, practical support to help you stay safer online, recognise scams, manage your privacy, and build healthier digital habits. Whether you're feeling unsure or want to sharpen your awareness, I'll guide you through it in a way that's calm, respectful and easy to understand.
